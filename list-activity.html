Scrambled Egg

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan
  2. Gently mix the eggs an d cream
  3. Add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs
  5. Remove from the heat
